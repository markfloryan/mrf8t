Summer 2017 Game Design Projects

Click on a link below to download a game. Games were developed in either Java or Javascript (developer's choice). For a Java game, simply unzip the archive and run the [gamename].jar file. For Javascript games, simply open the primary .html file in a web browser. Google Chrome is the only officially tested browser for these games.

*Note that some games require controllers to work, and may be unplayable without a properly configured controller.

One Life: Control four characters at once through platforming challenges! Don't lose your one life!!

DarumaKun: Help Daruma retrieve his other eye by bouncing along planets and collecting Konpeito candy!

Guardian of the Classroom: Help Floryan protect his classroom from ensuing zombies!

Parallax: Solve platforming puzzles by moving between planes in the environment!

Spash: Consume smaller objects while avoiding larger ones and collect all the mass in the universe!

Super Slash Balls: They may look like marbles, but are ready to battle in this super smash brothers style fighter! *Requires XBox controllers

Peak Performance: Race down the mountain on your skis! Can you get the fastest time?
